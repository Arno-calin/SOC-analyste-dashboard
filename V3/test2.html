What is Behavioral Rhythm?

Behavioral rhythm represents the fingerprint of your enterprise operations. By mapping events to time-of-day, SOC analysts can quickly distinguish between human error, maintenance artifacts, and genuine threat actor activity.

How to use this view

Dark blue areas indicate quiescence or standard background activity. Moving towards hot pink indicates peak intensity. Use "Historical Mode" to compare current data against the last 6 months of training data.
